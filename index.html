Bug Bounty Programs

A bug bounty program, also called a vulnerability rewards program (VRP), is a crowdsourcing initiative that rewards individuals for discovering and reporting software bugs.

Bug Bounty Platforms

About Platforms

Bugcrowd is a crowdsourced security platform. It was founded in 2011 and in 2019 it was one of the largest bug bounty and vulnerability disclosure companies on the internet

HackerOne is a crowdsourced vulnerability coordination and bug bounty platform, founded in 2012, that connects businesses with penetration testers and cybersecurity researchers.

Synack was founded in 2013, and according to Bloomberg "the most trusted crowdsourced penetration testing platform.".

SecureBug was founded in 2019, a crowdsourced platform for bugbounty hunting.
